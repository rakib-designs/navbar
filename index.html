Still got questions? I’ve got you covered!

What are your rates?

I have a broad range of services so I also have a very broad range for my rates. That's why I first jump on a call with my clients to know which services can help them the most.

What happens in a call?

The discovery call is a 20-minute free call that's used to help us BOTH know if we're a good fit for each other. I'll be asking you more about your business, goals and challenges. I'll also share my full process.

Do you do payment plans?

Smaller services like my intensive and consultation calls require a 100% upfront. Payment for bigger projects can be divided into 3 payments.

How can I trust you?

I totally understand! Collaborating with a complete stranger can be scary. Feel free to discuss all your concerns in our first call. I also create an Agreement that protects the BOTH of us during the entire project.
